projects

Gameshow

A NodeJS based gameshow which allows you to stream one client to a TV and control the gameshow using your phone. The question-sets will be stored in JSON files and can easily be customized.
The "master" can add questions to the gameboard, select them, start a timer, add players and assign them scores and more. All actions are displayed by the "display" client.

github.com/elYanuki/Gameshow

Revenge

An eight minute long short film I created with two friends in the span of 2 months for a school project.

Lego Stop-Motion

A short one minute Lego video that perfectly loops. We tried to make all animations as smooth and possible and perfect the scenery for each shot.
In the end we spent about 16 hours each, just moving minifigures by 3 millimeters and taking a picture. We then also coincidentally got into contact with a retired music professor from Michigan that was willing to create custom music for the video.

Stop-Motion video on YouTube

World Manager

An ElectronJS based desktop app meant to store a wiki and the corresponding map for fictional worlds.
You can create a world and upload an image to be used as the map, which you can add markers to. The markers come in 3 different sizes and can be moved around and renamed. When clicked they link to a wiki entry. The wiki is based on the editorJS libary and allows you to create new pages and add elements like headings, tables, lists and so on.

github.com/elYanuki/WorldManager

Norway

In the summer of 2022 my family and me visited Norway with our small camping van. We got there by ferry and drove around the southern part of the country for 3 weeks.
I recorded hours of video footage of the trip and cut it down to 15 minutes. Of course, I also added music, voiceovers, titels, color correction, etc. resulting in a nice video documentary of our journey.
